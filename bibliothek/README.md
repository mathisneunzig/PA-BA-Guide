# Bibliothek — Borrowing Guide for Students

Welcome to the Bibliothek lending system. This guide explains how to create an account, find books, and manage your loans.

---

## 1. Create an Account

1. Go to the site and click **Register** (top-right or the login page).
2. Fill in your first name, last name, a username, your email address, and a password.
3. Click **Register**.
4. You will receive an **email with a verification link**. Open the email and click the link.
   - Without this step you cannot log in.
   - If you did not receive the email, check your spam folder.
5. Once your email is verified, go to **/login** and sign in.

---

## 2. Browse the Catalog

The book catalog at **/books** is publicly visible — no account needed.

- Use the **search bar** to search by title, author, or keyword.
- Filter by **genre** or **language** using the dropdowns.
- Click on a book to see its detail page, including availability and a description.

---

## 3. Reserve or Borrow a Book

> You need to be logged in as a **student** to borrow books. Guest accounts cannot borrow.

1. Open a book's detail page.
2. Click **Reserve / Borrow**.
3. Choose a **start date** (the day you plan to pick it up) and a **loan duration** (up to 91 days / ~13 weeks).
4. Confirm the reservation.
5. You will receive a **confirmation email** with your due date.

Your loan will show up in **My Loans** with the status **Reserved**.

---

## 4. Picking Up a Book

When you come to pick up the book, the **librarian (Admin)** will activate your loan in the system. Your status will change from **Reserved** to **Active**, and you will receive a **receipt email** with the due date.

---

## 5. Returning a Book

Return the book physically to the library. The librarian will mark it as returned in the system. Your loan status will change to **Returned**.

---

## 6. My Loans

Go to **My Loans** (dashboard link) to see all your current and past borrowings:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Reserved | Booked, not yet picked up |
| Active | Currently with you |
| Returned | Successfully returned |
| Overdue | Past due date, not yet returned |
| Cancelled | Reservation was cancelled |

---

## 7. Email Notifications

You will receive emails for:

- **Reservation confirmation** — when you create a reservation.
- **Loan receipt** — when the librarian activates your loan.

---

## 8. Cancelling a Reservation

You can cancel a **Reserved** loan yourself from My Loans by clicking **Cancel**. Active loans can only be closed by returning the book to the library.

---

## 9. Account Settings

- **Profile** — update your name, username, and contact details.
- **Forgot password** — use the link on the login page to receive a password reset email.

---

## FAQ

**Can I extend a loan?**
Contact the librarian. They can extend the due date from the admin panel.

**How many books can I borrow at once?**
There is no hard limit set by the system, but the librarian may enforce a limit per student.

**What if a book has no copies available?**
You can still reserve it for a future start date. The system will tell you the earliest date a copy becomes available.

**I never got the verification email.**
Check your spam folder. If it is not there, contact the librarian to have it resent.
